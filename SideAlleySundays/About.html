About Side Alley Sundays

There is so much mug fuggin good music in this city, it seems a shame that there isn't one place to check it all out.

That’s the kind of shit we used to say... but no more!

Ben Scott and Davis Van Luven are a couple of cool dudes who realized that they have what it takes to make, let's call it, a repository of sweet, crunchy, local tunes. Something like that. At first, “what it takes” was a camera and mad editing/mastering knowledges, but those poor blokes would soon find that they had a manic urge to chill with and film the cream of the local music crop in the Emerald City (that’s Portland to the laymen).

So here it is, so far. And it will always be “so far” because the people in this city, hell, every city/town/municipality/county/shire/burg/pueblo /ville/commune/village/loose-conglomeration-of-shacks-on-some-weird-hill on this planet are a never ending garden of creativity!

So, take the time and take a listen. These are talented folks and so are you.

See what you like, and like what you see!

Thanks,
Ben and Davis
